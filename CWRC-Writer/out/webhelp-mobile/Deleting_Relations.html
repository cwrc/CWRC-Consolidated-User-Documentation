Deleting relations

If you have mistakenly added a relation, you can delete them in the left column.

There are two ways to remove relationships in CWRC-Writer.
  • Right-click on the entity that you would like to delete. Click on the Remove Relation option that pops up.

  • At the bottom of the "Relationship Column" there is a button called Remove Relation. Select the relationship that you would like to delete and click on that button.

This will remove any relationships that you have added to your document.
