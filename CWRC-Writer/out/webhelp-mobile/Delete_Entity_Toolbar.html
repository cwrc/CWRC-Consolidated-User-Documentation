Deleting entities from the toolbar

The CWRC-Writer templates come with pre existing entity annotations as example to new users. You can easily delete these annotations or any annotations that you don't want in your document from the toolbar.

  1. Highlight the text tagged with the entity annotation that you would like to delete.


  2. Click on the Remove Tag toolbar button.
CAUTION:
Currently, removing an entity from the document deletes the entity record in left column and the text tagged with it as well.
