Deleting entities from the entities column

The CWRC-Writer templates comes with pre existing annotated entities as examples to new users. You can easily delete these entities, any entities that you don't want in you document, or those you have mistakenly added from the entities panel.

Right-click on the entity that you want to delete in the left hand "Entities" column and choose the Remove Entity option.

This will delete both the entity record in the left column and the entity (with text) in the document.
