Sorting word frequencies

You can customize how you view word frequencies in DToC by different organizational headings and by how the words are displayed under those headings.

  1. Select the heading you would like to view the list of words organized by.
  2. Select how you would like the word frequencies to be organized by choosing one of the options available from the drop down menu.

  3. You can add more options by clicking on the black arrow menu option that appears beside the organization headings when you hover your mouse over it. This brings up a drop down menu, select the “Column” option that displays another drop down menu filled with additional heading options.
