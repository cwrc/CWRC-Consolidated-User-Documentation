Navigating the text area

The text is separated into chapters, sections, or articles by the XML document. The text area loads individual sections, so the whole document will not appear unless there are no text divisions in the document.

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the text in the text area.
  2. Click on "Next Chapter" or "Previous Chapter" to navigate through the document sections.
