Sorting Word Frequencies

You can customize how you view word frequencies in DToC by different organizational headings and by how the words are displayed under those headings.

  1. Select the heading you would like to view the list of words organized by.
    The initial options are: Type, Count (raw count of words), Relative (relative count of words), and Trend. You can add more options by clicking on the black arrow menu option that appears beside the organization headings when you hover your mouse over it. This brings up a drop down menu, then select the “Column” option that displays another drop down menu filled with additional heading options. Check any additional options you would like to organize word frequencies by.

  2. Select how you would like the word frequencies to be organized by choosing one of the options available from the drop down menu.
    The drop down menu is accessed through the black arrow menu option that appears beside an organization heading when you hover your mouse over it.The options are: “Sort Ascending,” “Sort Descending,” “Group By This Field,” and “Show in Groups.” Additionally, you can choose to reverse the default order by selecting the small black arrow (not the menu button) located directly next to the organization heading.

  3. Explore the list of words found in your document.
    To change back to the default options click on the “Reset” option located at the bottom of the column.
