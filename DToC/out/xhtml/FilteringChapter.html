Filtering by Chapter

In the left column on DToC you can sort options by a particular chapter. This is available for the “Index” and “Tags.”

  1. Click on the “Index” or “Tags” tab in the left column, depending on what you would like to filter by.
  2. Choose the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu. Select the chapter you would like a closer examination of. This will narrow down the index terms to those found in the selected chapter.
    You can move to different options in the left column (“Tags” or “Stats”), but they will not be filtered by the Chapter you have selected in the “Index” column (and vice versa). However, when you return to the “Index” tab, the chapter filter will remain selected.
  3. You can then select an index term or tag to see where they are located in the selected chapter.

  4. To reset the filter, click again on the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu and unselect the chapter you have selected.
