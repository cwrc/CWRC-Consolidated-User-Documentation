Commenting on text

You can add context to your annotations using the "AnnotateIt" tool. For more information about signing up to “AnnotateIt” see “Logging in to Annotator Authentication.”

  1. Log in to “AnnotateIt” and activate it in your DToC browser (See “Logging in to Annotator Authentication” linked below).
    Once you have activated “AnnotateIt” a banner will drop down from the top of the browser telling you that it is ready.

  2. Highlight the text you would like to comment on in the text area.
  3. Click on the small popup that appears over your cursor (it is represented by a pen and page).
  4. Write what you would like to annotate your highlighted text with.
    You can also add any keywords that you would like associated with the text you have highlighted.

  5. Once you have finished adding the information to your text click on “Save.” If you don’t want to annotate the text or you have changed your mind you can click on “Cancel” and your annotation will not appear.
  6. Optional: If you would like to delete or edit the annotation click on it and select one of those options in the top left corner of the popup.
    If you delete the annotation you will delete everything, comment and tag.
