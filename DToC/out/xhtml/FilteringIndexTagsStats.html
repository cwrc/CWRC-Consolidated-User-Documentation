Filtering Index terms, Tags, and Stats

The filter option allows you to search for a specific term in the index, tag, and stats coluns on the left side of the page.

  1. Type the term or tag that you would like to locate in the list. You can search for terms or tags found in a specific chapter by selecting an option from the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu.
    The terms are narrowed down automatically as you type. The filter narrows down terms or tags based on whether they have the specific combination of letters within the word (not just if the word starts with the letters you are typing).
    Note: The Stats column search filter is located at the bottom of the column.



  2. Type the term or tag that you would like to locate in the list. You can search for terms or tags found in a specific chapter by selecting an option from the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu.
    Terms are represented in orange, tags in blue, and words in purple.

  3. To restart your search choose the “Clear” button located at the top of the document model.
