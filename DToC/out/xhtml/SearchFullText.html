Searching the full text

You can use the DToC search function if there is a specific word you are looking for.

If you have selected a chapter from the Table of Contents then your search will only be conducted within that chapter. If you would like to conduct a full text search make sure that none of the chapters/sections are selected.

  1. Type in the word(s) that you are searching for.
    The search box is autocomplete-enabled.

    Search for exact words or phrases. For example, if a typo or extra space is included, your search may not work.
  2. DToC displays the location of the word you are looking for under the title of the chapter they appear in and in the document model. Search hits are represented in purple.
    You can scroll through the results in the "Table of Contents" using your mouse.

  3. To restart your search choose the “Clear” button located at the top of the document model.
