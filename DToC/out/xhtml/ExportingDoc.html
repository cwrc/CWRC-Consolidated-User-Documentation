Exporting your document

The Export option is used to create and preserve a link to the curated version of a document/edition.

  1. Click on the gear icon in the top right corner of the text column.
  2. A popup appears, select the “Export” button and the bottom of the popup.

  3. A second popup appears with a URL for your document. Click on “URL” (highlighted in blue) to open your document in a new window.

  4. Click on “Okay”, you will now have two versions of your document open.
