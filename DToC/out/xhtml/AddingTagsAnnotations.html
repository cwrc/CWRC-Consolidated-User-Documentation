Adding tags to text

Using the "AnnotateIt" tool allows you to manage your annotations, this includes changing who can view/edit annotataions or adding tags to the highlighted text.

  1. Log in to “AnnotateIt” and activate it in your DToC browser (See “Logging in to Annotator Authentication” linked below).
    Once you have activated “AnnotateIt” a banner will drop down from the top of the browser telling you that it is ready.

  2. Highlight the text you would like to tag in the text area.
  3. Click on the small popup that appears over your cursor (it is represented by a pen and page).
  4. Enter the tag that you would like to add to your highlighted text.
    You can also add a comment to the highlighted text in the same popup.
