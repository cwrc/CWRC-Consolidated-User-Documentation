Navigating Text in the Table of Contents

Users view text in DToC by looking in the right hand column of the tool. You can access the text in three different ways.

  1. Open a document in DToC.
  2. Click on a section/chapter/article in the Table of Contents.
    This will move the black arrow in the document model display area to the text that you have selected.

  3. Scroll to read.
