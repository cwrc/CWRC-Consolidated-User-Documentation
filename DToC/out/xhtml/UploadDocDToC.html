Uploading your XML document to DToC

Uploading the document that you have created into the Dynamic Table of Contexts involves adjusting XPath settings in DToC.

http://voyant-tools.org/?skin=dtc

  1. Open up the settings in the homepage of DToC. This option is represented by a gear in the top right corner of the “Add Text” box.

  2. Change the “Input Format” to TEI.
  3. Enter this XPath information into the corresponding areas:
    DToC accepts an URL, a local file, or a string as input. DToC splits the XML into "documents" based on XPath criteria that is provided when importing the file into DToC. What we've used in the past is an XPath that matches <div type="chapter"> and <div type="index">. It assumes that all documents (except for the last) are chapters. The last document is assumed to be the index for the entire XML file.

    Each chapter should have author and title info. When importing, you can provide XPaths to these values, relative to the parent chapter.

    For more information on XPaths see http://www.w3schools.com/xsl/xpath_intro.asp

  4. Optional: The XPath to document section assumes that your document is organized into chapter(s), a forward, and an index. You may have to configure the XPath to document section if your document has additional structure components (such as an afterword).
    To add an after word, you would need to add this expression to the larger chain:

    ‘afterword’=@*[local-name()=’type’]

    The Xpath to Documents chain would now look like:

    //*[local-name()='div' and('chapter'=@*[local-name()='type'] or 'forward'=@*[local-name()='type'] or ‘afterword’=@*[local-name()=’type’] or 'index'=@*[local-name()='type'])]


  5. Optional: Click "OK".
  6. Optional: Click on the “Upload” button in the lower right corner of the “Add texts” box.
  7. Optional: A popup appears. Click on the “Add” option at the top of the popup.

  8. Optional: Select the XML document on your computer that you would like to upload to DToC.

    If you accidently uploaded the wrong document click on the left icon (with the red delete symbol) and upload the correct document. Or you can upload more than one document, but only one with be “Revealed.”
  9. Optional: Once the document appears in the upload popup, click on “Reveal.” Voyant will process your document. Once it is finished processing your document, DToC will open with your XML document uploaded.
