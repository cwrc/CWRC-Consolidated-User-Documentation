Exporting your document

  1. Click on the gear icon in the top right corner of the text column.
  2. Select the “Export” button at the bottom of the popup.



  3. A second popup appears with a URL for your document. Click on “URL” (highlighted in blue) to open your document in a new window.



  4. Click on “OK”, you will now have two versions of your document open.
