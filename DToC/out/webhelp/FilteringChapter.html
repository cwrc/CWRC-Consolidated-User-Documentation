Filtering by chapter

When you move between different tabs in the left column they will not be filtered by the Chapter you have selected in the previous column. However, when you return to the initial tab, the chapter filter will remain selected.

  1. Select the "Index" or "Tags" tab in the left column.
  2. Choose the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu. Select the chapter you would like a closer examination of. This will narrow down the index terms to those found in the selected chapter.
  3. You can then select an index term or tag to see where they are located in the selected chapter.



  4. To reset the filter, click again on the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu and unselect the chapter you have selected. Or click on the "Clear" button above the document model.
