Viewing Stats by Chapters and Collections

  1. Select an option to view the word frequencies. You can either choose to view words by individual chapters or by the entire document.

  2. Optional: If you have selected to view words found in chapters or sections, you must then select the chapter you want from the table of contents. Click once on the chapter you want to work with.
    When you enter the “Stats” tab or select the “Chapter Stats” option, the first chapter is the default view.

  3. Optional: If you have selected to view the words by collection (which refers to the entire document with all chapters), continue to explore the listed words.
