Uploading Text and URLs into DToC

Some features will be unavailable using this method (like the index).

  1. Paste your text or an URL into the text box located on the front page of DToC.

  2. Click on the “Reveal” button located right below the text box. This opens the document in DToC.
  3. Begin to work with your text.
    Using this method, you will not have an index, tags or search functionality (even if you paste in an XML document). The table of contents and the document model will also not work. However, you will be able to see information in the stats column (term frequency).
