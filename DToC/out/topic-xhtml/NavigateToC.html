Navigating Text in the Table of Contents

The Table of Contents displays terms selected (from the left column) and lets users move through the text by clicking on them. This allows for in-depth exploration of terms without scrolling through large chunks of text.

  1. Click on a section/chapter/article in the Table of Contents.
    This will move the black arrow of the document model to the text that you have selected.

  2. Scroll to read.
