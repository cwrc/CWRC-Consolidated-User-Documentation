View Edges

Edges are displayed both in the Graph and in the Commands Tab.

When a user hovers overtop of a connective line, a label appears indicating the nature of the relationship and the number of connections in this category.

Edge can also be viewed in the Commands Tab. The Edges of Selected Nodes bin contains the names of Edges (or relationship types) associated with any nodes in a Selected state.
