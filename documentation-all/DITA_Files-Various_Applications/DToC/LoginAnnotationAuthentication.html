Logging in to the annotator

  1. Click on the Annotation tab or “Export” button within the annotation tab to produce an “Annotator Authentication” popup.
  2. Click on the “Annotator Login” button.
  3. If you have an account, login, if you don't follow the instructions provided by “AnnotateIt.”
  4. Once you have an account with “AnnotateIt” bookmark the site to your browser bookmark bar.

  5. Reload your DToC document and click on the “AnnotateIt” bookmark on your bookmark bar.
  6. Begin to annotate your document in the text area.
