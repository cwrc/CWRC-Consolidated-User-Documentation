Messaging project members

You need to be logged in to your CWRC account to send messages.
  1. Click on the bell icon, located in the top right corner of CWRC.
    Clicking on the bell icon takes you to your message inbox.

  2. Click on the New Messages button.

  3. Fill out the new message form.
    To send to other users you need to enter their user name, however CWRC provides drop down menu of user names based on what you begin to type (see screenshot below).

  4. Optional: To preview your message click on Preview Message .
  5. Click on Send Message . You will get a banner notification that your message was sent.
