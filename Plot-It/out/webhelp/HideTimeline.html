Hiding the timeline

If you are not interested in using the timeline, or would like to just focus on the map/list/grid, you can hide the timeline.

  1. Click on the “Hide Timeline” option, above the timeline on the left side.
  2. Optional: When you are ready to view the timeline again, select “Show Timeline”. This is located in the same upper left corner that the “Hide Timeline” option was.
