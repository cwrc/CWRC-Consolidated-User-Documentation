Filter by Date of event range

The “Date of event range” allows users to adjust what events users see between a specific period of time.

  1. Toggle the date range using the two arrows available. The text boxes at the bottom of the page display the exact year that the arrows have settled on.

    When the user moves the white arrows along the date range, the years will automatically change in the text boxes below. Additionally, users can type the years in manually (in the text boxes).


  2. View the events that are between that date range in the timeline or the map.
