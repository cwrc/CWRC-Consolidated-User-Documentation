Reset filters

Users can reset both a search and searching filters in multiple ways. Once a user has reset or unselected the checkboxes, the timeline and map will re-populate with all events.

  1. Click on the "Reset Search Filters" above the map in Plot-It.

    The “Reset All Filters” option only appears once the users has conducted a search or has selected a filter. This is the only way to reset Plot-It after a search.


  2. Optional: Additionally, you can uncheck the top checkbox (or individually selected boxes) of the filters.
