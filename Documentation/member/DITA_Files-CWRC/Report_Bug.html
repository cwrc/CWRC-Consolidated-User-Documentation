Report a bug

CWRC is still in development and you may discover some bugs. Please let us know if you find an issue.

  1. Click on the question mark icon in the top right corner.
  2. Click on Report a Bug.
  3. Fill out the Report a Bug form.
    Please provide as much detail as possible. This will help our programmers to easily find, reproduce and fix the bug.

  4. Click Submit.
