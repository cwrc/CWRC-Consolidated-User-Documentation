Viewing stats by chapters and collections

  1. By default, word frequencies are provided for the entire document. You can choose to view term frequencies by each individual chapter by selecting one from the "Filter by Chapter" list.

  2. Optional: If you have selected to view words in an individual chapter and you wish to return to the stats of the entire corpus, you must click on the chapter that has a checkmark beside it in order to deselect it.
