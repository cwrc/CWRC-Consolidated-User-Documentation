Annotating your document

CWRC-Writer provides users with the opportunity to annotate documents. This feature is only available to users who are in the same project as the document or if the document is unlocked.

  1. Click on the "Annotate" tab.
    You cannot annotate your document if someone else is editing/annotating it or if it is locked (unavailable to non project members). You will see error messages (and you will remain on “View” mode).



  2. Begin to annotate your document by adding person, place, organization (etc.) entities to it.
  3. Click on “Save” or “Save and exit” if you are done annotating your document.
