Walk Mode

Pulls connected nodes in the graph; keeps previously clicked nodes.

In walk mode, every node you click becomes the central node in the graph. Nodes immediately connected it will be drawn into the graph.

Walk mode illustrates the user's "pathway." Previously clicked nodes will turn black and remain in the graph; all other periferral nodes will be re-shelved.
