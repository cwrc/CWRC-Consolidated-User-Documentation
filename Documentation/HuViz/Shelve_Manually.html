Shelve Manually

Shelve nodes using the "drag and drop" method with the following steps.

  1. Click and hold the node, illuminating the green Shelf.
  2. Drag the node to the Shelf.
  3. Release the clicker to drop the node.
The node "Quebec" has been returned to the Shelf and is no longer in the central Graph.
Note: In the previous example, the node was dragged to the Shelf from the central Graph. However, nodes can also be shelved from the Discard bin.
