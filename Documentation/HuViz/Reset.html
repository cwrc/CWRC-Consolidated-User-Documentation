Reload and Restart

Reload the dataset or Restart from home.

Use the Commands Tab to Reload the dataset OR to Restart the app from the hompage. Both buttons are located at the top of the Commands Tab beside the titles of the dataset and the ontology.

  • Click the "cycle" sign to Reload the dataset.

  • Click the "x" to Restart and clear the graph and return to home.

Note: Reloading or Restarting the app will automatically erase all progress.To save your work use the "Save Script" functions in the History Tab.
