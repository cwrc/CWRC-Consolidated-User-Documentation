Save Session to File

Exports a record of the user's actions.

The Command History can be exported as a .txt file.

  1. Click the History Tab to view the Command History.
  2. Click the download arrow to export the History as a .txt file.
  3. Choose a filename and click "OK."
    Your session is downloaded as a .txt file.
