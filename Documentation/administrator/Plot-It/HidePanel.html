Hiding the panel

If you would like to work only with the timeline, or are not interested in the map/list/grid panel, you can hide that information.

  1. Click on the “Hide Panel” option, above map/list/grid panel on the left side of the page.

    Once you have hidden the panel, the legend that explains what the coloured icons refer to disappears as well.

  2. Optional: When you are ready to view the timeline again, select “Show Panel”. This is located below the timeline on the left side.
