Filtering index terms, tags, and stats

The Search feature narrows down terms automatically as you type. The string of characters being entered into into the Search bar will bring up words containing that string, regardless of whether the word begins with those characters or not.

  1. Type the term or tag that you would like to locate in the list.

  2. You can search for terms or tags found in a specific chapter by selecting an option from the “Filter by Chapter” drop down menu.

    Terms are represented in orange, tags in blue, and words in purple.



  3. To restart your search choose the “Clear” button located at the top of the document model.
