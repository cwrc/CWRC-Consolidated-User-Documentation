Navigating the document model

The Document Model is a deconstructed version of your document. Each section of grey lines represents a different section, chapter, or article within the document.

  1. Click on a section in the Document Model.
    The Table of Contents and the title at the top of Reading Pane will tell you what section you've selected.

  2. Work with the "Index," "Tags," and "Stats" columns and see what lines are highlighted.

  3. When you are done working with the Document Model, or when you would like to start a new search, click on the "Clear" button at the top of the Document Model.
