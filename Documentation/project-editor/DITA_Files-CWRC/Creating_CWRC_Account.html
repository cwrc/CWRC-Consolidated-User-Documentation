Creating a CWRC account

CWRC provides a customizable user experience for members. Members of CWRC can upload, edit and explore digital texts.

  1. Click on the Login/Join button in the top right corner.
  2. Fill out the Create new account form.

  3. You will be brought back to the CWRC homepage. A thank-you for applying for a CWRC-Writer Membership.
    CWRC will send you an email if you are approved for an account.
