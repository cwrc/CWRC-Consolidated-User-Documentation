Exporting your document

  1. Click on the grey circle icon in the top right corner of the text column.
  2. Select the “Export” button at the bottom of the popup.

  3. A second popup appears with a URL for your document. Click on “click on this link” (highlighted in blue) to open your document in a new window.

  4. Click on “OK." You will now have two versions of your document open.
