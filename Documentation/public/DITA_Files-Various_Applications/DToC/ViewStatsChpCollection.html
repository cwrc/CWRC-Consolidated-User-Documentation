Viewing stats by chapters and collections

  1. Select an option to view the word frequencies. You can either choose to view term frequencies in the entire document, or by each individual chapter.

  2. Optional: If you have selected to view words found in chapters or sections, you must then select the chapter you want from the Table of Contents. Click on the chapter you want to work with.
    When you enter the “Stats” tab, the first chapter is the default view.

  3. Optional: If you have selected to view the words by collection, which refers to the entire corpus, you may continue to explore the words as they are listed in the "Stats" tab.
